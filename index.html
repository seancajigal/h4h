What happens in the first session?
+
The first session is really about getting to know each other. We'll talk about what's brought you to therapy, what you're hoping to change or understand, and a little of your background. There's no pressure to share more than you're comfortable with. At the end, we'll discuss whether working together feels like a good fit.
How long will I need therapy?
+
It varies enormously from person to person. Some focused issues can be addressed in 8‚Äì12 sessions; deeper patterns or trauma often benefit from longer work. We'll regularly check in on your progress and goals, and you're always free to end or pause at any time.
Is everything I share truly confidential?
+
Yes, absolutely. Everything you share stays between us and is protected by professional ethical standards. The only exceptions are rare legal requirements ‚Äî such as if there is a serious and imminent risk of harm to yourself or others ‚Äî and we will always discuss these limits with you at the start.
Do you offer online sessions?
+
Yes. Online sessions via secure video are available and are just as effective as in-person for most concerns. Many clients find them more accessible and equally meaningful. You can choose your preferred format, or mix both depending on your week.
